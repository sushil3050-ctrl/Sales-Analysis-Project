*Turning data into insights*

A Python project for analyzing historical sales data and forecasting future sales using **time series forecasting**.  
It provides insights on top products, top customers, sales by region, monthly trends, and predicts the next 6 months of sales.

---

## Features

- **Data Cleaning:** Removes duplicates and fixes date parsing.
- **Monthly Sales Analysis:** Aggregates monthly sales and visualizes trends.
- **Top Products & Customers:** Identifies top-selling products and highest-spending customers.
- **Sales by Region:** Visualizes sales distribution across regions.
- **Forecasting:** Predicts next 6 months of sales using **Holt-Winters Exponential Smoothing**.
- **Visualizations:** Line charts, bar charts, and pie charts for easy analysis.
